Sloth

Hibiscus is to them what chocolate is to us

  • Scientific Name: Bradypus
  • Average Height: 23-27 inches
  • Average Weight: 15-18 pounds
  • Habitat: Tall trees in rain forests

Their lack of movement acts as a defense mechanism against predators. Sloths are slow that algae grows on their fur, serving as a camouflage for them! Sloths live entirely in trees, only coming to the ground for bathroom breaks. Sloths only take bathroom breaks about once a week! On rare occasions, they've been seen moving as fast as cats.
